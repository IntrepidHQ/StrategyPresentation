### Product lines relevant to Saunders

- **WOOD doors & drawer fronts** — butt joint, miter joint, veneer. Species: Maple, White Oak, Rift White Oak, Walnut, Alder, Cherry, Red Oak, plus Maple/Hardwood paint grade. See `02-decore-doors.md`.
- **DECO-FORM®** — thermofoil / Supermatte / Suedette routed or solid drawer fronts. See `02-decore-doors.md` (Deco-Form section).
- **Finishing** — Solvent-based clear, stain, primer, paint (Sherwin-Williams palette). See `03-decore-finishes.md`.
- **Drawer boxes** — Dovetail plywood (659), Doweled plywood (649), Dovetail solid maple (613). See `04-decore-hardware.md`.
- **Hardware** — Salice soft-close undermount & side-mount glides, Salice compact & traditional hinges, knobs & pulls. See `04-decore-hardware.md`.

### Lead times & warranty

| Line | Lead time | Warranty |
|---|---|---|
| DECO-FORM | 6 working days | 5-Year Limited |
| WOOD (unfinished) | 6 working days | 1-Year Limited |
| WOOD (finished) | 15 working days | 1-Year Limited |

### Quality notes

- Decore recommends ordering a **ColorFirmation sample block** before committing a paint color to a full order — catalog printing and natural wood variation mean the real finish won't match the brochure exactly.
- Rubber glass stops are standard on glass-door variants (e.g., `520X`, `521X`, `508G`).
- Hardware is Salice; model codes and sizes are documented in `04-decore-hardware.md`.
