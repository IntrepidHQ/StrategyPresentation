# Decore.com — Finishing

All wood styles can be ordered Finished or Unfinished. Finished adds **~9 working days** to the lead time (21 total vs. 6). Finishing is solvent-based, done at Decore's facility (Monroe, NC).

> **Always recommend a ColorFirmation sample block** before committing a paint color. Natural wood variation + catalog printing means the final doors won't perfectly match the brochure.

Order-form selection is: **Finish Type → Finish Name → Sheen**.

---

## Sheens (pick one per order)

| Finish type | Available sheens |
|---|---|
| Clear / Stain | Matte 10 Sheen · Satin 35 Sheen |
| Paint | Matte 10 Sheen · Satin 30 Sheen |
| SBF | Satin Sheen SBF · Matte 10 Sheen SBF (callouts on the order form) |

---

## 1. Clear (Natural)

Applied to Maple Finish Grade only.

- **Clear** — brings out the natural grain; no pigment.

---

## 3. Primer (paint base)

Applied under a paint finish on Hardwood Paint Grade. Available tones:

- White
- Gray
- Black

Primer choice affects final paint opacity and depth — dark paints typically use gray or black primer.

---
