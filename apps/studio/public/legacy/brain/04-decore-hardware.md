## Ordering rules of thumb

- Match glide length to drawer-box depth (not face width). A 22" drawer box uses a 22" side-mount or 23-5/8" undermount.
- Traditional / inset doors use the `7821` hinge; everything else defaults to `7802`.
- For prefinished drawer boxes, confirm the edge-banding direction before order submission — it matters on taller boxes.
- Hardware SKUs should be captured on the intake so the automation can line-match them when the quote goes out.
